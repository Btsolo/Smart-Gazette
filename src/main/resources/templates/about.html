About Us

Smart Gazette is a civic information platform focused on helping young people in Kenya stay updated on government decisions and policies. It works by automatically retrieving new Kenya Gazette publications, summarizing them into short, youth-friendly articles using AI, and sharing the content on a website and social media.

This system solves the problem of important government updates being hard to understand or easy to miss. By making the information easier to access and digest, Smart Gazette encourages public awareness and participation, especially among youth. This project is developed in partial fulfillment of the degree of Computer Science.
